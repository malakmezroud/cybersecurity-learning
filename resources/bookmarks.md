## Certifications Roadmap

### Entry Level
- **CompTIA Security+** - Industry baseline, widely recognized
- **CompTIA Network+** - Networking fundamentals
- **Cisco CCST Cybersecurity** - Cisco's entry cert
- **Google Cybersecurity Certificate** - Coursera, beginner-friendly

### Intermediate
- **CompTIA CySA+** - Blue team / SOC analyst
- **CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker)** - Pentesting concepts
- **eJPT (eLearnSecurity Junior Penetration Tester)** - Practical pentesting

### Advanced
- **OSCP (Offensive Security Certified Professional)** - Gold standard for pentesters
- **CISSP** - Security management, architecture
- **CISM** - Security management
